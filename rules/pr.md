---
description: PR conventions for clear, reviewable pull requests
---

## Naming

PR title describes the feature added, not the PR number or phase:
- Good: "App shell with state management and UsernameModal"
- Bad: "PR 12: App shell" or "Phase 7: Frontend scaffold"

## Scope

Each PR is one logical change, independently reviewable, and leaves the app in a working state. No mega-PRs.

## Body

Explain what the change does and what works after merging. Bullet points are fine.
